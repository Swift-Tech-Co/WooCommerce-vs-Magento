When to choose which
Use-case recommendations from the full analysis
Small to mid content-driven store (under 5,000 SKUs) WooCommerce

Lower cost, faster setup, WordPress content integration, sufficient for most small stores

Enterprise B2B catalog (10,000+ SKUs, complex pricing) Magento (Adobe Commerce)

Native multi-store, shared catalogs, company accounts, and enterprise-grade performance

Blog-heavy brand with e-commerce secondary WooCommerce

WordPress is the dominant CMS; WooCommerce slots in natively

Multi-country, multi-currency retailer Magento (Adobe Commerce)

Native multi-website architecture; built-in currency and language management

Budget-conscious startup WooCommerce

Near-zero initial cost; host on shared for under $20/mo; scale when revenue justifies it

Custom checkout and complex promotions Magento (Adobe Commerce)

Native promotions engine, customer segments, and cart price rules exceed WooCommerce
